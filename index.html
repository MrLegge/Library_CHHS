Welcome

This is a resource to guide you with your inquiry learning. It will assist you to locate, evaluate and use information wisely.

It recommends the Guided Inquiry Design Process as an effective way to complete research tasks.

Year 7 Guided Inquiry Booklets use this approach.

Access resources by selecting your year group and then subject.

Each subject page will offer:

  • Information about the assessment task with links to relevant documents
  • General information, recommended websites and suggestions for searching
  • A list of possible topics with links to appropriate resources
