About the task:

Research a Francophone country from the list. Check the task description and note-taking scaffold so you know what information is needed.

The Teacher Librarian will guide you during three lessons in the library. She will help you with locating and evaluating information, note-taking, bibliography writing and preparing your pamphlet.

Finish the task at home. Hand in both your Guided Inquiry Booklet and your pamphlet. Do your best by checking that your work matches the marking guidelines.
