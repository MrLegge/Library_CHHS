About the task:

Research a marine mammal from the list. Check the task description and note-taking scaffold so you know what information is needed.

You will work on the project in class with your teacher. You can also use this resource at home or in the library where Mrs Rienstra can help you with locating and evaluating information, note-taking, bibliography writing and preparing your power-point, poster or model/report.

Finish the task at home. Hand in both your Guided Inquiry Booklet and your presentation. Do your best by checking that your work matches the marking guidelines.
